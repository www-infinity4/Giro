Core Concept

What is Giro?

Giro fuses two physical disciplines — gyroscopic dynamics and cutaneous vascular regulation — into a single closed-loop control system for precision biomedical applications.

🌀

Gyroscopic Inertia Pool

A spinning rotor maintains angular momentum proportional to its angular velocity. The "pool of inertia forces" describes the conservation of this momentum within the mechanical frame, providing a stable reference for detecting perturbations.

🔴

Erythema as a Signal Source

Erythema — skin redness caused by capillary dilation — is "specially sourced" via optical or infrared sensors to produce a real-time vascular-activity signal. Intensity and spatial distribution encode the severity and location of inflammation.

⚡

Symptomatic Regulation

The regulator compares the erythema signal against a setpoint and drives a corrective actuator — mechanical vibration, thermal or pneumatic stimulus — to bring tissue back within healthy bounds, completing the closed-loop feedback cycle.

📡

Operational Mode

"Operational" emphasises continuous, real-time activity. The system samples the erythema field at high frequency, updates the inertial reference, and issues corrective commands without human intervention.

Mechanism

How Giro Works

Five interdependent subsystems cooperate to deliver continuous regulation.

01

Optical Erythema Sensing

A spectrophotometric or infrared array captures the spatial heat-map of surface redness at up to 60 Hz, producing a continuous intensity field E(x, y, t).

02

Gyroscopic Reference Frame

A MEMS gyroscope trio provides angular-velocity vectors (ωx, ωy, ωz), anchoring the erythema map to body-fixed coordinates despite patient movement.

03

PID Regulation Loop

A proportional-integral-derivative controller computes an error signal ε = E − Eref and outputs a corrective drive waveform to the haptic/thermal actuator.

04

Actuator Response

Targeted micro-vibration, localised cooling, or pneumatic compression reduces capillary pressure and erythema intensity at precisely the identified site.

05

Adaptive Learning

Session data trains a lightweight model that personalises setpoints and gain parameters over successive treatments.
